Culture Push Portfolio

Christina Cappelli

01

Transference

Photograph selected from the Transference Series: Mexico that traces how traditional practices of coloration, fermentation, and material interaction embody collective memory and the passing of land-based knowledge from expert to novice. Research and Documentation were archived in San José Deguedo, Mexico with Raul Ponton.

02

Liminal Lens

An interactive installation that visualizes the collective movements of Volvox algae to reveal the hidden rhythms of microscopic life. Translating microscopic movements into immersive visuals, it invites viewers to reflect on scale, perception, and our entanglement with microscopic life. Computational Video created in openFrameworks ( c++ ).

03

Proxima Kòsmos

image select from the Proxima Kósmos series created with The Berggruen Institute. This is an internal render of a speculative terrain, shaped through the research of planetary scientists and astrobiologists at institutions such as MIT Media Lab, NASA, and ASU. I created the renders using generative design, adobe create suite and components tested in Blender.

04

Biome Boba

Image still from a speculative synthetic biology project. Inspired by extremophiles, this research explores microbial adaptation in extreme environments and investigates how microbial ecosystems from specific body biomes could be leveraged for novel therapeutic applications—such as integrating personalized living cultures into boba—reimagining the future of personalized wellness. Conducted at Genspace as part of MIT’s How to Grow Almost Anything course.

05

The Void

An interactive exploration of digital identity exposing a feedback loop where identity is shaped by the systems mediating it. Computational Video created in openFrameworks ( c++ ).
